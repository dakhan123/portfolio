JournalXplorer

JournalXplorer is a smart and user-friendly mobile application designed to help researchers, students, and academicians quickly search and explore ABDC (Australian Business Deans Council) journals.

The app eliminates the need to manually browse large datasets or spreadsheets by providing a fast and efficient search experience. Users can easily find journals using title, ISSN, ranking, or field of research.
