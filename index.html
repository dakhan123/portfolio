About JournalXplorer

JournalXplorer is AcadXAI's flagship research discovery product. It indexes over 120,000 peer-reviewed journals across disciplines and uses large language models to help researchers find the most relevant venues for their work — whether they're submitting a manuscript, conducting a systematic review, or simply exploring a new field. The platform provides impact metrics, open-access filters, editorial timelines, and AI-generated journal summaries, all in one place.

Key Features

Semantic Journal Search

Describe your research in plain language and surface the most relevant journals instantly.

Impact & Metrics

Access CiteScores, Impact Factors, h-index data, and acceptance rate estimates.

Open Access Filter

Filter by Gold, Green, or Diamond OA status to fit your funding requirements.

Review Timelines

See crowdsourced and AI-estimated peer review durations before you submit.

Smart Watchlists

Save journals to personalised lists and get notified of new calls for papers.

Export & Cite

Export journal metadata in BibTeX, RIS, or CSV for seamless reference management.

Privacy Policy
JournalXplorer Privacy Policy

JournalXplorer respects your privacy.

  • The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.
  • No login or registration is required.
  • The app works completely offline and does not require an internet connection to access journal information.
  • We do not use cookies, tracking tools, advertising networks, or third-party analytics services.
  • Any journals saved as favourites and app preferences are stored locally on your device only and are never shared with us or any third party.
  • JournalXplorer does not access your contacts, photos, files, location, camera, microphone, or any other personal information on your device.

Data Sources & Disclaimer

The journal information provided in the app is sourced from publicly available datasets and is intended for informational and academic purposes only. While efforts are made to keep the information accurate and up to date, users should verify journal details from official sources before making publication or research decisions.

Affiliation Disclaimer

This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially associated with the Australian Business Deans Council (ABDC) or any journal ranking authority.

Future Updates

Any future updates that involve data collection or usage will be clearly communicated through an updated Privacy Policy.
